Privacy & Cookie Policy

◆

Last updated: 12 July 2026

This page explains what personal data LUMIÈRE collects through this website, why, how long we keep it, and the rights you have over it under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), as Cyprus is an EU member state.

1. Who we are

LUMIÈRE, 14 Amathountos Avenue, Limassol, Cyprus, is the data controller for information submitted through this website. For any privacy request, contact privacy@REPLACE-WITH-YOUR-DOMAIN.com or call +357 94 032 850.

2. What we collect

  • Reservation form: full name, email address, preferred date, and party size.
  • Newsletter form: email address only.

We only collect what's needed to manage your booking or subscription — nothing more.

3. Why we collect it

To respond to and manage reservation requests, and to send the monthly newsletter to guests who opt in. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Who processes it

Form submissions are processed by Formspree, our third-party form backend, which delivers the submission to us by email and stores it on our behalf. Formspree acts as a data processor under our instruction. Review Formspree's own privacy practices at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

5. How long we keep it

Reservation details are kept for 90 days after your visit, then deleted. Newsletter email addresses are kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR, you can ask us to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to how your data is used

To exercise any of these, email privacy@REPLACE-WITH-YOUR-DOMAIN.com. You can also lodge a complaint with the Cyprus Commissioner for Personal Data Protection.

7. Cookies & tracking

This website does not currently use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technology. The only third-party requests it makes are to Google Fonts (for typefaces) and Unsplash (for some imagery) — neither sets tracking cookies for this site.

If we add analytics (e.g. Google Analytics) in future, this section will be updated first, and a cookie consent banner will be added before any non-essential cookie is set.

8. Security

Data in transit is protected via HTTPS. Form input is validated and length-limited on both the browser and Formspree's side, and every field is rendered as text only — never as executable markup — on this site.

Questions about this policy? See our Terms & Conditions or contact us directly.
